Every email you get has hidden structure inside it. A person, a request, a deadline, an action. You feel it every time you re-read the thing at 4pm trying to remember what it actually was.

You could capture that structure. Type it into a list, a tracker, a CRM. Most people don't, because sitting there converting prose into fields is worse than re-reading the prose.

There's a "friction tax" that gets paid twice. Once on the pile you never capture. Once on the conversion don't have energy to make. Every "just use this system" productivity pitch has been bouncing off that wall forever.

What changed: we now have systems that can make the conversions. The structure that was already there gets surfaced without you sitting there extracting it.
